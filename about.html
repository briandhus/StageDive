About Stagedive

There are plenty of apps dedicated to finding out where your favorite band is playing, so why use Stagedive?

Stagedive allows you to find your favorite band and plan your escape from your boring life. When you enter the name of a band and your location, we'll display a map showing your location and the venues where your favorite band is playing. Stagedive will also list all of the venues where your musical heroes will be playing and list the corresponding dates.

You say you’ve seen this trick before... This is where Stagedive takes a simple search for bands and turns it into a truly awesome adventure. Stagedive does a comprehensive search of flights near you and returns the lowest fare results for your inquiry for a few days before and a few days after your concert date. You have a vacation planned and you get to see your favorite band! Don’t worry, you can come home after your incredible adventure and still watch the paint dry or throw rocks at the trains.
